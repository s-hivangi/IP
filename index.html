Featured Hotels

The Glen Lodge

The Glen Lodge offers a cozy retreat for outdoor enthusiasts.With easy access to a network of trails, guests can immerse themselves in the stunning landscapes of the surrounding mountains, forests, and streams.

Hôtel Le Marais

Hôtel Le Marais is a charming boutique hotel located in the heart of the historic Marais district. With its elegant décor and modern amenities, this hotel offers a comfortable and stylish stay just steps away from Paris's iconic landmarks.

The Hotel Peninsula

The hotel features elegantly designed rooms with stunning city views, state-of-the-art amenities, and a serene spa. Guests can enjoy exquisite dining options, including a rooftop restaurant that offers panoramic views of Tokyo.

Featured Activities

Guided Hiking Tours

Join our guided hiking tours that explore the breathtaking trails of the Catskills. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced hiker, our knowledgeable guides will lead you through scenic routes, showcasing the area's rich flora and fauna. Experience the tranquility of nature and capture stunning views, making it an unforgettable outdoor experience.

Seine River Cruise Tour

Experience the beauty of Paris from the water with a scenic Seine River cruise. This guided tour allows you to take in iconic sights such as the Eiffel Tower, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and the Louvre, all while enjoying a leisurely ride. With audio commentary available, you'll learn about the rich history of the city as you glide past its most famous landmarks, making for a memorable addition to your Parisian getaway.

Underwater Diving

Venture to the Izu Peninsula, a short trip from Tokyo, renowned for its beautiful dive sites. Join a guided diving tour to explore vibrant marine life, underwater caves, and colorful coral reefs. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced diver, the Izu Peninsula offers breathtaking underwater scenery that will make for an unforgettable adventure during your stay in Tokyo.
